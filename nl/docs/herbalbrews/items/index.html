Tea‚Äã

The core of this mod: The various variations of tea.

Each tea offers a different effect. Some types of tea can be placed for decoration purposes. Teas are crafted in a Tea Kettle.

Flasks‚Äã

Flasks behave similarly to Potions - they are drinkable and mainly provide bonuses to damage and armor. There are both small and large flasks; however, these only differ in the duration of the effect.

Flasks are crafted in a Brewing Cauldron.

Can be obtained by harvesting wild coffee. Usable as seeds for Coffee Plants.

Lavender Blossom -

Can be obtained by harvesting Lavender with shears.

Tea Blossoms -

Can be obtained by harvesting flowers with shears. Usable as seeds for Tea Plants.

Green Tea Leafs -

Can be obtained by harvesting flowers with shears. Usable as seeds for Tea Plants.

Dried Green Tea -

Can be obtained by drying a Green Tea Leaf Block

Dried Black Tea -

Can be obtained by drying a Dried Green Tea Leaf Block.

Dried Oolong Tea -

Can be obtained by drying a Mixed Tea Leaf Block.

Completionist Banner‚Äã

The Completionist Banner is awarded to all players who have crafted all Tea Variants from the HerbalBrews Mod.

Note: To receive the respective Advancements, you need all the items in your inventory at the same time.
